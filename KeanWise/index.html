Academic Information

• Access to Self Service Portal: Register and manage your Kean University courses. View progress towards program completion

• Access to My Profile details: Ability to view Personal Information such as Full Name, Address, etc.

• Access to Official Transcript Request Portal: Ability to officially request your Kean University transcript

• Access to Kean Graduation Portal: A portal used for upcoming Kean University graduates to register for graduation



• Access to View/Pay My Bill Portal: Ability to pay your tuition bill and access to financial costs

• Access to Meal Plan Options: Ability to enroll yourself into a meal plan option program

• Flex Dollar Transfer: Acesss to the flex dollar transfer system for Kean University

• Access to Self Service Portal: Ability to accept, deny and manage financial aid that is awarded

• Access to ARP Application Portal: Ability to apply for ARP grants

Other Information

• Access to Residence Life Housing Portal: Register and apply for Kean University Resident Housing assistance program

• Access to Student Records Release Form: Ability to withdraw education records from Kean University based on Student ID

• Access to Proof of Insurance Page for Resident and International students: Ability to access form for proof of insurance

• Access to Emergency and Missing Student Contact Form: A form to complete in case of missing student emergencies



• Access to What's My ID Form: A form that assists students with retrieving their Kean Student ID

• Access to What's My Password: A form used to retrieve a forgotten Kean University KeanWise password

• Access to Change My Password Form: A form that allows students to reset their KeanWise Portal password

• Access to Address Change Form: A form that can be used to change your current living/mailing address
